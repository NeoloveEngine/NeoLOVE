# Entity Methods Sample

This sample demonstrates:

- `entity:Listen(...)` / `entity:listen(...)`
- `connection:Disconnect()`
- `entity:AddComponent(...)`
- `component:Remove()`
- `entity:Delete()`
- `entity:Duplicate(...)`
- `entity:FindFirstChild(...)`
- `entity:GetWorldPosition()` / `entity:GetWorldRotation()`

Controls:

- Hover the blue card on the left.
- Left click: move the card and fire two listeners.
- Right click: duplicate the badge.
- Middle click: remove or restore a text component.
- Scroll up/down: rotate the orange dial on the card.
- Click the green button: disconnect the secondary left-click listener.
- Click the red chip inside the card: delete that entity.
